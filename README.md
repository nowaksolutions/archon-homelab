<h1 align="center">A.R.C.H.O.N Homelab</h1>

<p align="center">
  Advanced Reconnaissance & Control Host Operations Network
</p>

<p align="center">
  <i>Segmented, monitored, and instrumented environment designed to emulate enterprise security operations and defensive architecture.</i>
</p>

---

<h2>Overview</h2>

<p>
This homelab is a structured, security-focused environment designed to simulate real-world enterprise infrastructure. It is built to support hands-on experience in network defense, log analysis, threat detection, and system hardening aligned with industry standards.
</p>

<p>
The environment emphasizes visibility, segmentation, and control, enabling practical exposure to SOC workflows and defensive security operations.
</p>

---

<h2>Core Infrastructure</h2>

<p>
<strong>Network Architecture</strong> - Segmented topology utilizing ISP gateway, dedicated firewall, and managed switching to enforce traffic control and isolation boundaries.
</p>

<p>
<strong>Virtualized Hosts</strong> - Windows and Linux systems deployed for endpoint simulation, attack emulation, and log generation.
</p>

<p>
<strong>Domain Services</strong> - Centralized authentication and policy management through a domain controller to replicate enterprise identity infrastructure.
</p>

<p>
<strong>Monitoring & Telemetry</strong> - SIEM-integrated logging pipeline for event aggregation, correlation, and detection engineering.
</p>

<p>
<strong>Raspberry Pi Monitoring Node</strong> - Lightweight health monitoring and network visibility system providing real-time operational awareness.
</p>

<p>
<strong>Storage & Resilience</strong> - NAS for centralized storage and UPS-backed power continuity to maintain uptime and data integrity.
</p>

---

<h2>Security Focus</h2>

<p>
<strong>Framework Alignment</strong> - Designed in alignment with NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) and SCAP-informed configuration baselines.
</p>

<p>
<strong>Detection Engineering</strong> - Log generation, parsing, and analysis to support threat identification and behavioral analysis.
</p>

<p>
<strong>Network Defense</strong> - Firewall rule tuning, segmentation enforcement, and traffic inspection.
</p>

<p>
<strong>Operational Simulation</strong> - Emulates SOC workflows including alert triage, incident investigation, and response validation.
</p>
